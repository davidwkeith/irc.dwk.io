# Seasonal content suggestions

Get timely blog post ideas based on your business type and upcoming events.

## How it works

The agent checks a seasonal marketing calendar with hundreds of content hooks organized by month, business type, and lead time. It filters for events relevant to your business that are coming up in the next 2–3 weeks, and suggests blog post topics.

## Examples

- "Valentine's Day is in 20 days — florists often see engagement from posts about arrangement previews and order deadlines."
- "It's tax season — a good time for a post about your document checklist."
- "Spring cleaning season is starting — a 'book your spring deep clean' post could help fill your schedule."

## When suggestions appear

- At the start of a session (if your business type is configured)
- When you ask "what should I post?" or "any content ideas?"
- After a health check as a "while you're here" idea

## Customization

Suggestions are based on your `BUSINESS_TYPE` in `.site-config`. If your business spans multiple types (e.g., a restaurant that also does catering), set them comma-separated: `BUSINESS_TYPE=restaurant,hospitality`.

## Calendar source

The seasonal calendar covers all four quarters with universal hooks (relevant to every business) and industry-specific hooks for 50+ business verticals. It includes lead time guidance so suggestions arrive early enough to act on.
